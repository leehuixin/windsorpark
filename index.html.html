About Us

With greater efforts to encourage people start living a more active lifestye, Windsor park was set up to provide people from all age groups a rejuvenation point for friends and family members to bond together.

History:

Preservation of green buffers has been one of the steps put forward by National Board Parks (NParks). With existing trails repaired, new trails and raised boardwalks were added to trail network, giivign rise to 3 new routes- Venus Loop, Drongo Trail and Hanguana Trail
